About CSHS

The Broad Run Computer Science Honor Society (CSHS) is a prestigious organization dedicated to recognizing students who demonstrate excellence in computer science, leadership, and community service. Founded in 2018, our chapter at Broad Run High School fosters a passion for technology and innovation while encouraging members to make a positive impact in their school and local community.

CSHS members participate in a variety of activities, including organizing coding bootcamps, mentoring middle school students through Hour of Code initiatives, and collaborating on tech-driven community service projects. Our society emphasizes not only technical proficiency but also teamwork, communication, and ethical leadership in the ever-evolving field of computer science. By joining CSHS, students gain access to exclusive workshops, networking opportunities with industry professionals, and a platform to showcase their skills through regional and national competitions.

Why Join CSHS?

Becoming a member of the Broad Run Computer Science Honor Society is an opportunity to immerse yourself in the world of technology while making a tangible difference. CSHS offers a dynamic environment where you can enhance your coding skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and build lasting friendships. Members enjoy exclusive access to workshops, hackathons, and mentorship programs designed to prepare them for college and careers in STEM fields. Beyond academics, CSHS encourages community involvement, helping students develop leadership skills and a sense of responsibility that will serve them well in any path they choose.

Join us to unlock new opportunities, challenge yourself, and be part of a community passionate about computer science and service.
